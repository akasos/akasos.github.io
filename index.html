About

"May your blades never dull."-World of Warcraft
Hi, my name is Austin Stephens, I’m a kid who transitioned his obsession for gaming into a passion for computer science. Discovering my partiality for computers wasn't easy. After major-hopping a few (several) times, I took a programming class and quickly identified my desire to learn the language of the machine. Currently, I work as a software engineer developing avionics software at Hill Air Force base in Layton, Utah. I graduated this year (May 2018) with my bachelor's degree in computer science. I enjoy front and back-end web development, JavaScript and programming in Java. I enjoy working with others, and I pride myself on being a perfectionist. I believe in taking the time to do something right, the first time. Offline I try and step away from the screen for a change. I enjoy running long distance, fiddling on the piano, listening to alternative music and hiking in St. George. Going forward, my aim is to be challenged daily, fulfilled by the complexity of my career, and continually motivated by time-constrained deadlines. I'm eager to broaden my knowledge, dabble in my adjacent fields, and expand my love for computers.
